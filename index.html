I am a first-year PhD student at Department of Computer Science, School of Engineering, the University of Manchester, supervised by Prof. Wei Pan and Prof. Michele Caprio.
I am also working as a remote research intern at Humanoid Robot Motion Control Department, X-Humanoid.
I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science from Peking University in July 2025, and was recognized with the EECS Top Ten Outstanding Undergraduate Thesis Award.

My research intersts lie in robot learning, especially that for Humanoids. My research aims to build general purpose robots, which can really be help of people's everyday life.

Other Collaborators: Shanghang Zhang, Qiang Zhang, Jian Tang, Lin Shao, Bin Zhu, and Jiahuan Zhou.
